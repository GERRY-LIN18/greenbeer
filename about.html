The GREENBEER story

Becoming the Taiwan's leading premium lager

It’s been 10 years since GREENBEER has been able to call itself a microbrewer. And while we’re proud that 10 thousand GREENBEER’s are served each day across 10 cities, we’ve never forgotten that the quality is measured not in the size of our brand but in the purity of our beer. Even as we continue to open up to new worlds, our uncompromised drive for quality and perfection still lives on in the GREENBEER family.
